Privacy Policy — Veil Run

Last updated: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how VoxDuru ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game Veil Run (the "App"), available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personally identifiable information (PII) directly. The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data automatically:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS / GAID on Android)
  • Device type, OS version, language, and time zone
  • IP address (used to determine general location)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

2. How the Information Is Used

Data collected by AdMob is used by Google to serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements and to measure ad performance. We do not use this data for any other purpose. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties beyond AdMob.

3. Google AdMob

The App uses Google AdMob to display rewarded advertisements. AdMob is operated by Google LLC. For more information on how Google processes data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Data Usage

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

5. Data Retention

We do not store any user data on our servers. Any data collected by AdMob is subject to Google's data retention policies.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data (e.g., access, deletion, opt-out of personalized ads). To opt out of personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
